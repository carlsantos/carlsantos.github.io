The Analytical Data Method

Adcrft’s method to converting the best customers starts with data, evolves with data and is measured with data. Yes, it’s a lot of data. But it’s data that ensures the strategy we create is based on how well we know your audience, how we expect them to engage with the campaign, how we design and continually optimize the creative and ultimately how we define and prove success.

Creative Driven By The Data Scientist

The Creative is In the Data. We extract the data that tells your story. Your data is unique. So are your customers. The creative that leads to more conversions is derived by the opportunities in your 1st party data combine which we combine with 2nd and 3rd party data sources to gain the insight that drives the creative strategy.

Programmatic Media Buying Beyond Acquisition

Targeting the right customer at the exact right time via the right mix of media throughout their buying journey is how we gain attention, clicks and conversions. We produce omni-channel campaigns across mobile, video, search, social, display, audio, connected TV (OTT), out of home (OOH) and native. We consult with your crm and sales teams to seek automation opportunities, ensure healthy lead scoring and to optimize ROI. We don’t stop at acquisition. We also focus on the NetPromoter Score to help you maximize your customer relationship and overall Lifetime Value (LTV).

Holistic & Transparent Campaign Performance Reporting

We constantly review and report on your entire campaign performance holistically across all channels – search, social, programmatic and direct buys from creative to conversions. You will always know what is working and what needs enhancing.

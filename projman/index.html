PHCA is a Day Care school located in San Fernando, Pampanga that teaches Nursery, Kinder I and Kinder II.

ABOUT

Potter’s Hand Christian Academy is a Day Care school in San Fernando, Pampanga that teaches Nursery, Kinder I and Kinder II. The academy also have three teachers and two admins that maintains the school. It also have facilities that can help a student to learn. The classroom of the school also have corners, wherein the corners have different sections where children can build knowledge and improve their skills. PHCA is a friendly school where children won’t be just focusing on academics but how to fear God as well.

PHILOSOPHY

  • Unique - Child grow physically, emotionally, cognitively according to his own pace of development.

  • Self-Propelling - Child needs the freedom to choose his own work for individual pride, dignity, and integrity.

  • Gifted - A child endowed with different skills and talets.

  • Special - Created accordig to the image of the Creator.

VISION and MISSION

  • To Provide quality CHRIST centered education for young children at affordable cost.

  • To provide opportunity for holistic growth and development for young children.

  • To provide loving and safe environment where children can experience, security.

  • To promote the welfare of children and their families through social development programs.
